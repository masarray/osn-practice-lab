# Product Strategy

## Product principle

OSN Practice Lab SD should help a child feel safe to try, calm during CBT practice, and curious enough to return.

## Learning loop

```text
Daily Mission → Gentle Feedback → Review Wrong Answers → Reward Ticket → Short Mini Game → Next Mission
```

## Anti-frustration rules

- Do not shame wrong answers.
- Prefer “belum tepat” over “salah”.
- Keep daily mission short.
- Keep serious simulation separate from reward/gameplay.
- Show parent-facing analytics without making the child feel compared to others.

## UX modes

- Daily Mission: 10 questions, no harsh countdown.
- Try Again: wrong questions and weak topics first.
- OSN Simulation: 60 questions, 60 minutes, CBT-like.
- Adventure Map: topic-based missions.
- Reward Room: short controlled mini games.
